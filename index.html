Hello, I'm Ahamad Khan

Khan Aluminium Fabrication is a trusted name in aluminium fabrication and interior solutions across Corlim, Goa. With over a decade of hands-on experience, we deliver premium-quality doors, windows, railings, glass partitions, kitchen cabinets, and full interior fabrication for homes and commercial spaces alike. Our commitment to precision craftsmanship, durable materials, and timely delivery has earned us the trust of 500+ satisfied clients. From a single window replacement to complete turnkey interiors — every project is handled with the same care and dedication, under the direct supervision of owner Ahamad Khan.
